Designing environments for people, culture and change.

SDE is an international design studio based in Berlin.

We create buildings, pavilions, workplaces and cultural environments that connect architecture, technology and human experience.

We believe that meaningful design emerges when people, nature, culture and technology are considered as part of one interconnected environment.

Our work spans architecture, exhibitions, workplaces and destinations across Europe, the Middle East, Asia and beyond.
